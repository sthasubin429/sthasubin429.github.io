How to play?

Player 1

  • W /A /S /D To move
  • U /I /O for Low/Medium/Heavy Punch
  • J /K /L for Low/Medium/Heavy Kick
  • M for Special Move

Player 2

  • ← ↑ ↓ → Arrows To move
  • Numpad 7 /8 /9 for Low/Medium/Heavy Punch
  • Numpad 4 /5 /6 for Low/Medium/Heavy Kick
  • Numpad 1 for Special Move
